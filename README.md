# Zombie-Car-Driving

Car Driving & Zombie Survival Game

Race against time in this thrilling survival game! Drive your car, dodge obstacles, escape zombies, and reach the finish line before it’s too late. Can you survive the chaos and cross the line to victory?
🕹️ How to Play

    Drive Smart: Use controls to steer, accelerate, and brake.
    Dodge Obstacles: Avoid barriers and traps on your path.
    Escape Zombies: Stay clear of zombies trying to stop you.
    Reach the Finish Line: Survive the challenges and win the race!
